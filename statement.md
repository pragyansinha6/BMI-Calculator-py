# Statement of the Project

## Problem Statement
Many people struggle to easily calculate their Body Mass Index (BMI) and understand their health category based on weight and height. Manual calculations can be error-prone and confusing due to unit conversions and formula complexity.

## Scope of the Project
This project aims to provide a user-friendly web application that calculates BMI accurately by accepting weight, height, and age inputs in various units and returning the BMI value along with a health category. The app will convert units as needed and validate user inputs.

## Target Users
- Health-conscious individuals seeking quick BMI calculation  
- Fitness enthusiasts and trainers  
- Medical practitioners needing a simple tool for BMI estimation  
- Anyone interested in monitoring their weight health status

## High-Level Features
- Input for weight, height, and age with unit selection  
- Automatic conversion between pounds/kilograms and inches/centimeters  
- BMI calculation using the standard BMI formula  
- Clear categorization of BMI results into health categories  
- Validation of inputs to ensure positive numbers  
- Simple and intuitive web interface using Flask, HTML, and CSS
